Privacy Policy

Zentrack app

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Important: This document is provided for general information. It is not legal advice. If you need advice for your situation, consult a qualified attorney.

Zentrack (“the App”) is published by Shivansh Dengla (individual developer). This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in connection with the App. By using the App, you acknowledge this Policy.

Short summary (App Store / Google Play)

Content you enter in Zentrack stays on your device; we do not operate a backend that stores your tasks, notes, or similar app content. Payments for subscriptions are processed by Apple or Google; we do not receive or store your full payment card number. Subscription validation may use RevenueCat, which processes limited purchase-related data as described below.

1. What we do not do

  • We do not require you to create an account with us to use the core App.
  • We do not operate our own servers to store your in-app content (such as tasks, notes, or logs you create in Zentrack).
  • We do not collect or store your full payment card number; card data is handled only by Apple, Google, and their payment processors.

Apple and Google may collect information according to their own policies when you use their platforms. You are responsible for reviewing their terms and privacy notices.

2. Data on your device

Information you choose to enter in the App is stored locally on your device, unless you use features provided by your device or platform (for example, iCloud or Google backup), which are outside our control.

  • Your responsibility: You control your device, backups, and security (passcode, device encryption, etc.). You are responsible for safeguarding your device and any copies of your data.
  • Deletion: Removing data from the App or uninstalling the App may delete local data; we cannot recover data from your device for you.

3. Subscriptions and payments

Zentrack may offer paid subscriptions (for example, “Zentrack Pro” or similar) through the Apple App Store and Google Play. When you subscribe, Apple or Google processes the payment. We do not receive or store your full payment card number.

What we may receive (limited)

We may receive limited information related to your subscription, such as:

  • Subscription status (for example, active, expired, trial, canceled)
  • Product identifier (for example, monthly or yearly plan)
  • Purchase or renewal dates
  • Country or region associated with the store account (as provided by the platform)

Trials

If you start a free trial, the store may require a valid payment method. Billing typically begins when the trial ends unless you cancel in time, according to that store’s rules. Trial availability and length are shown in the App and/or on the store listing.

Cancellation

You cancel or manage your subscription in your Apple ID or Google Play account settings. Canceling stops future charges; access may continue until the end of the current paid period, depending on store policy.

4. RevenueCat (subscriptions)

We use RevenueCat to help manage subscriptions, validate purchases, and reflect subscription status across devices. RevenueCat may process information such as:

  • App user identifiers used for purchase validation (for example, an anonymous app user ID)
  • Subscription and entitlement status
  • Technical data needed to operate the service (for example, app version, platform)

How RevenueCat handles data is described in its privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Data minimization

We design the App so you do not enter card details inside Zentrack for subscriptions. Payment details are handled by Apple or Google.

Paid features are not intended for children under the age required by your region without appropriate parental consent. If you believe information has been collected from a child inappropriately in connection with the App, contact us using the details below.
