Privacy Policy

ZenTrack Application

Effective Date: January 1, 2023

Welcome to ZenTrack! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

ZenTrack is designed to function without requiring personal data collection. However, we may collect the following information for app functionality and improvement:

  • User-Provided Information: If you voluntarily enter data such as notes, expenses, workouts, or food logs, it is stored locally on your device.
  • Analytics Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app's performance and features.
  • Device Information: Non-personal data such as device model and operating system may be collected for troubleshooting and app optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and enhance ZenTrack's features, including Notes, Finance Tracking, and Health Tracking.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience.
  • To troubleshoot issues and optimize performance.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All user-entered data (notes, expenses, workouts, and calorie tracking) is stored on your device and is not uploaded to any server.
  • Security Measures: We take reasonable security precautions, but we encourage users to back up their data independently.

4. Third-Party Services

ZenTrack does not share your personal data with third parties. However, we may use third-party analytics tools that collect anonymized data for app improvement.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time within the app.
  • Since data is stored locally, uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app, and continued use of ZenTrack constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at shivanshdengla@gmail.com.

By using ZenTrack, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
